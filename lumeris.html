Lumeris

Site Management


Lumeris is a healthcare company that manages several different brands across various markets. I built and managed an e-commerce storefront that allowed Lumeris's brokers to order marketing and enrollment materials to sell Medicare and health plans.

I worked directly with the client to implement Single Sign-On (SSO) and SAML solutions into the storefront. This increased the security of the site and gave the client the ability to control access to the storefront.

Every year, the client prepares for the annual enrollment period. I project-managed the implementation of new product additions, UI/UX updates, and testing to ensure the site launched by the enrollment period.
