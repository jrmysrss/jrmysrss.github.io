Lumeris

Site Management


Lumeris is a healcare company that manages several different brands and across different markets. I built and and managed e-Commerce storefront that allowed Lumeris' brokers to order marketing and enrollment materials to sell madicare and health plans.

I worked directly with the client to implement Single Sign On and SAML solutions into the storefront. This increased security of the site as well as gave the client the ability to control access to the storefront.

Every year the client prepares for the annual enrollment period. I project managed the implementation of new product additions, UI/UX updates, and testing to ensure the site launches by the enrollment period.
