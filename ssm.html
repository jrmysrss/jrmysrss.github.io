SSM Health

Brand & Site Management


SSM Health is a healthcare and hospital system in the Midwest region with over 1,000 locations. The storefront was created to host the client's marketing materials, educational materials, and supply and medical forms. This allows users to order the necessary materials for the correct brand.

With SSM Health being such a large entity, I assisted the marketing team with managing the brand guidelines and implementing them across materials designed in-house for print as well as the storefront. I also developed a workflow for creating SSM Health's new logo packages during the rebranding process. This allowed for consistent logo designs across sub-brands, hospitals, medical practices, and locations.
