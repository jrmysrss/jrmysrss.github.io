Epsilon

Design Implementation & Site Management


Epsilon is a data-first digital marketing solutions company with a presence in three countries. A storefront was created to give their internal employees a place to buy company-branded promotional items as well as redeem anniversary and new hire gifts.

Epsilon provided designs created by their internal design team, and I implemented the design as a site theme in the e-commerce solution utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. During the development process, I guided the client's design decisions to help create a cohesive user experience for their employees on both desktop and mobile platforms.
